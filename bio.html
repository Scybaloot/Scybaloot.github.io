Bio

I am currently a UI/UX designer at Vecna Technologies where I define the functionality of products through research and iterative design. I graduated from Olin College, where I earned a BS in Engineering and saw the value of user-centered design.

My design training has been mostly user-centered design, with an emphasis on usability and needs-identification. However, recently I have been studying information design and web development. In my free time, I also like to cook, paint, and play Go.

If you want to learn more about my previous work or discuss design opportunities, feel free to message me on LinkedIn or email me at cdjsato@gmail.com.
