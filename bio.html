Bio

I am currently a UI/UX designer at Vecna Technologies where I define the features of products and the feasibility of different solutions. I graduated from Olin College of Engineering, where I earned a BS in Engineering and developed a deep appreciation for the design process.

My design training has been mostly user-centered design, with an emphasis on usability and needs-identification. However, recently I have been studying information design and collaboration technology. As is evident in my portfolio, I am obsessed with food. It tastes good, I love to cook, and it has deep ties to culture and sustainability. I also like to paint and play Go.

If you want to learn more about my previous work or discuss design, feel free to message me on LinkedIn or email me at cdjsato@gmail.com. This current site is under construction, so please see my portfolio site for more samples of my work.
