I took Human Factors in Interface Design and learned about wireframing and iterative design. My team and I designed a tablet app that helps people organize cooking events with their friends. The goals of the final design are to remind users of their events and inspire the creation of new ones.
My team and I studied dinner preparation on a stovetop and learned something surprising. The food cooked on the stovetop requires significantly more resources to produce than the stovetop. With this knowledge in mind, we designed an app "remixes" recipes to replace meat with "samples" of different foods.
